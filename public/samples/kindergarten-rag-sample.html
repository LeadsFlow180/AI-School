Little Stars — Our Day at School

This is a short sample story for teachers. You may print this page to PDF and upload it to test RAG in AI-School. The text below is original and not from any published book.

Mia and Leo are in kindergarten. They hang up their coats and say good morning to Ms. Rivera. They sit in a circle on the rug. Today they learn about the weather. The sun is bright, so they draw yellow rays on their papers. They count clouds: one, two, three. At snack time they wash their hands and share apple slices. After recess they read a book about a friendly frog who likes to hop. Ms. Rivera asks, “What did the frog do?” Leo says, “He hopped to the lily pad.” Mia adds, “He made a new friend.” Before they go home, they sing a song about kindness and tidy the blocks. Ms. Rivera reminds them: “We take care of our classroom and each other.”

Words to practice: sun, cloud, hop, friend, kind, share, wash, count (1–5), circle, listen.

Simple activity: Draw your favorite weather. Tell a grown-up one kind thing you did today.
